Rules for Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple game played by 2 people in the form of a 4x4 grid. One of the players plays O and the other plays X.

  • The game is played on a grid that's 4 squares by 4 squares.
  • The game is suitable for 2 player.
  • The first player is O and the second player is X
  • The first player to get 4 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  • When all 16 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 4 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
